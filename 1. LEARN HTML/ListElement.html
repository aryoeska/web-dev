Aryo's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 2 cups (480 ml) whole milk, warm
  • 1/2 cup (115g) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 5 cups (625g) all-purpose flour, divided
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt

Filling:

  • 3/4 cup (150g) brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened

Frosting:

  • 4 ounces (115g) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup (60g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup (120g) powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

Prepare the Dough:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the warm milk, melted butter, and sugar. The temperature of the milk should be warm but not hot (about 110째F/43째C).
  2. Sprinkle the yeast evenly over the warm mixture and let it sit for about 5 minutes until the yeast is frothy.
  3. Add 4 cups of flour to the yeast mixture and stir until just combined. Cover the bowl with a damp towel and set aside in a warm place to rise for 1 hour until doubled in size.
  4. After 1 hour, remove the towel and add an additional 1 cup of flour, baking powder, and salt. Stir well until fully incorporated.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the brown sugar and cinnamon.
  6. Turn out the dough onto a lightly floured surface and roll into a large rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick.
  7. Place the sliced rounds, slightly apart, in a buttered baking dish. Cover with a damp towel and let them rise for another 30 minutes.
  8. Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C).
  9. Once the rolls have risen, bake them for about 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  10. While the rolls are baking, beat together cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt in a bowl until creamy.
  11. Remove rolls from oven and while still warm, spread generously with cream cheese frosting.
